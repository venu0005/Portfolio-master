Hello! I'm Abhijith, a recent graduate from Algonquin College with a degree in Interactive Media Design. My passion for design and creativity drives me to explore the endless possibilities within Adobe Creative Suite, where I've honed my skills in graphic design, web design, and icon creation.

Before my college journey, I delved into the world of basic website development and graphic re-design, laying a strong foundation for my creative endeavors. Photography and video editing are close to my heart; I love capturing moments and crafting compelling stories through my lens. With experience in video editing and a keen interest in exploring more advanced software and coding languages, I'm always eager to expand my skill set.

I thrive in dynamic environments and am adept at adapting to new challenges. My ability to learn quickly and adapt to various situations has been instrumental in my growth as a designer. I look forward to bringing my skills and enthusiasm to new projects and continuing my journey of creative exploration.
